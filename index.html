Frequently Asked Questions

PMU Hair Stroke FAQ

Can I go back to normal activities right after PMU treatment?

+

Yes, you can. Hani's Hair Stroke technique minimises skin damage, so your brows won't look overly dark or swollen right after treatment. You can go to work or meet friends the same day — the results look natural immediately.

How long does hair stroke PMU last?

+

Results typically last 1.5 to 2 years depending on your skin type and lifestyle. Oily skin may fade slightly faster than dry skin. Retouches are available while Hani is based in London (until 2028).

Does PMU eyebrow tattoo hurt?

+

Most clients say the discomfort is minimal — many even fall asleep during the procedure. We use a high-performance, low-irritation numbing system, and the shallow, precise technique means even those with low pain tolerance can feel comfortable.

What pigments are used for the brow tattoo?

+

We exclusively use premium natural pigments certified by KFDA (Korea Food & Drug Administration) and European safety standards. These pigments maintain their true colour over time without turning red or blue.

Can I get PMU if I have old brow tattoo residue?

+

It depends on the depth and colour of the residual pigment. Please send an unfiltered photo of your brows before booking. Light residue can often be naturally covered with Hani's technique, though very dark residue may require laser removal first.

How does retouch work while Hani is in London?

+

Greenit's Hair Stroke technique is designed for high retention with just one session, and many clients are satisfied without a retouch. Since Hani is based in London until 2028, you can easily book a retouch session anytime.

Why is a deposit required?

+

Hani operates a private studio with limited slots (3-4 clients per day) to ensure the highest quality. The £100 deposit prevents no-shows and ensures that those who truly want the service have the opportunity.

When can I wash my face or exercise after PMU?

+

Avoid getting your brows wet on the day of treatment. From the next day, gentle face washing is fine. Avoid heavy exercise, saunas, and swimming for about one week to allow the pigment to settle properly.

What is the difference between hair stroke PMU and microblading?

+

Hair Stroke PMU (nanobrows) uses a digital machine for ultra-fine, hair-like strokes with precise depth control. Unlike microblading which uses a manual blade, Hair Stroke PMU causes less skin trauma, heals faster, and produces more consistent, longer-lasting results. It's especially suitable for sensitive skin.

How much does PMU cost in London?

+

Hair Stroke (Nanobrows) treatment at Greenit London costs £490, which includes a full consultation. The session takes approximately 2 hours. A £100 deposit is required to secure your booking via bank transfer or cash.
